What's Behind the Unique Flavors of the Bite Craft Burgers?

Our burgers comes with a thick and juicy patty without any sauce (none necessary), cheddar cheese from Italy, caramelized onions, and homemade buns.

A step up again in burger standards is the succulent and tender. This burger is not your usual kind of meat patty combination due to the quality of the delicious meat which has made BiteCraft popular.

The meat is hung for a minimum of 10 days before being specially prepared by the in-house butchers.
